Before you connect your real records

Plain answers to the questions that matter. Take the 30 seconds.

  • You're using your own right of access. You are retrieving your own health records and pointing them at a tool you chose. CareAgents is not your doctor and not a hospital — different rules apply than you may be used to.
  • Where your records live: behind the HealthClaw guardrail layer, in a private space scoped to you — with identifier redaction on reads and an audit log of every access. CareAgents itself stores no health data — only your account and a pointer to that space.
  • What the AI sees: redacted records, when a question needs them. Your data is never used to train models and never sold.
  • The AI can be wrong — and your connected records may be incomplete. Don't treat either as a substitute for your official records or for professional medical judgment.
  • Leaving: email support@healthclaw.io and we'll delete your data and confirm when it's done.
