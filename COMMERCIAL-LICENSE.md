HealthClaw Guardrails is **Fair Source** software, licensed under
[FSL-1.1-MIT](LICENSE) (the Functional Source License with an MIT future
grant — the model used by Sentry and others).

## What you can do for free

Almost everything:

- Use it internally at any organization — including hospitals, clinics,
  health systems, startups, and other commercial entities
- Use it for education, research, and personal projects
- Build applications and services **on top of it** (your patient app, your
  care-coordination service, your clinical workflow)
- Modify it, redistribute it, and offer professional services around it

## The one thing that needs a license

Offering HealthClaw itself — or a substitute for it — **as a commercial
product or service** (for example, selling a hosted "guardrails for health
AI agents" platform built from this code). That is the FSL's "Competing Use"
restriction, and it is the only restriction.

If that's you, we'd genuinely like to talk: **license@healthclaw.io**.

## Two years later, it's MIT

Each release automatically converts to the plain **MIT license on the second
anniversary of its publication**. Nothing is locked away forever.
Versions **v1.7.0 and earlier** were released under MIT and remain MIT
(see [LICENSE-MIT-v1.7-and-earlier](LICENSE-MIT-v1.7-and-earlier)).
